# くらメモ ブランド・レイアウトルール

## Brand

- Site name: くらメモ
- Tagline: 注目商品チェック
- Role: SNS・ニュースで話題の商品やサービスを、買う前に比較できるメディア
- Promise: 話題だけで決めず、条件・用途・価格・注意点で選びやすくする

## Visual direction

Use a refined, clean, slightly editorial design.

Preferred feel:

- White / off-white / pale blue / soft lavender
- Large but controlled serif headings
- Rounded cards
- Light gradients
- Clean product imagery
- Strong spacing, but not empty-looking
- Mobile-first hierarchy

Avoid:

- Generic corporate landing pages
- Too much blank space without purpose
- Dense text walls
- Same-size cards with no visual priority
- “広告っぽすぎる” banner dumping
- Random icons instead of product images

## First view

The first view must answer:

- What topic is this?
- Why should I care now?
- Where should I click first?

For top page, emphasize:

- SNSとニュースの話題を先取り
- 今注目されている商品やサービス
- 話題だけで決めず納得できる一品へ

For category pages, avoid vague text such as “このカテゴリでは比較します”.
Use direct naming:

- 通信・学び・お金
- 美容・脱毛
- 家事・時短
- 食・宅食
- 防災・備蓄

## Cards

Every visible card needs:

- Clear title
- What problem it solves
- Why it is timely or useful
- One concrete check axis
- Image or meaningful visual mark

Avoid cards that only say “比較” repeatedly.

## Product and banner images

- Product images should be large enough to recognize at mobile size
- Use images to break long service pages
- Banners can be used, but add short context: who it is for and what to check
- Do not rely on banners alone for important service pages

## Character usage

Use Kurara and Mikeru as guides, not mascots pasted everywhere.

Kurara:

- Trend context
- Why it is talked about
- Comparison framing
- “買う前に見るところ”

Mikeru:

- Specs
- Fees
- Review/condition checks
- Official condition reminders

Rules:

- Do not repeat the same portrait every few lines
- Do not put image-internal speech bubbles when the site already has a bubble
- If a bubble is to the right of a character, the character should face or gesture toward it
- Use casual spoken wording, but avoid repeated boilerplate like “ここは私と一緒に見てみよう”
- Top page and categories should include the guide characters lightly so the brand feels consistent
