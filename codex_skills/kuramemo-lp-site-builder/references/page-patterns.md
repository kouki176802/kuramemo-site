# くらメモ ページ構成パターン

## Top page / LP

Recommended order:

1. Hero
   - SNS・ニュースの話題を先取り
   - What the site does
   - Featured topic or product
   - Primary CTA: 話題の商品を見る
   - Secondary CTA: カテゴリから探す
2. “どこでなぜ注目されているか”
   - Domestic trend
   - Overseas trend when relevant
   - News/SNS reason
3. Guide library
   - Main categories with images
   - Avoid splitting closely related categories too finely
4. Service/value categories
   - 通信・学び・お金
   - 美容・脱毛
   - 家事・時短
   - 食・宅食
5. Site promise
   - 未使用商品を体験談化しない
   - 買わない選択肢も書く
   - 価格・条件はリンク先確認

## Category page

Recommended order:

1. Category hero
   - Direct category name
   - What can be compared
   - No unnecessary “公開中のガイド x本” panel unless useful
2. Guide cards
   - 2〜4 major comparison pages
   - Each card must explain the problem, not only list product names
3. Picked items / 注目候補
   - Product or service candidates
   - Explain why each is relevant
4. Buyer’s filter
   - 3 checks before buying/applying
5. Related guide links

Avoid:

- “次に広げるテーマ” when it does not help the user
- Vague category introductions
- Cards with repeated product names

## Product comparison page

Recommended order:

1. Hero with specific product group
2. Quick recommendation by use case
3. Product candidate cards
   - 8 items when possible
   - Product image
   - Price/review if verified
   - Why it is listed
   - Who it is for
   - What to check
   - CTA
4. Comparison table
   - Do not include useless columns like “状態”
5. Trend evidence
   - Explain where/why related demand exists
   - Overseas country should be named when relevant
6. Purchase checklist

## Service comparison page

Service pages must be more specialized than product pages.

Recommended order:

1. Search-intent hero
   - Example: 光回線おすすめ比較 2026
   - Promise: monthly price is not enough; compare total cost and conditions
2. “3つの質問”
   - Help the reader narrow candidates
3. Comparison table
   - 6〜8 major companies, even if some are not affiliate-approved
   - Include major names for credibility
   - Remove low-value columns like “確認先”
4. Candidate-by-candidate detail
   - Summary
   - Who it is for
   - Strengths to check
   - Cautions
   - Who should avoid it
   - Link / ad disclosure
5. Contract checklist
6. Ad banner section
   - Smaller banners
   - Short explanation for each banner

## Article copy pattern

Good section flow:

- 先に結論
- どんな人に向いているか
- 選ぶ前の注意点
- 比較表
- 候補ごとの説明
- 申込前 / 購入前チェック

For service pages, write long enough to satisfy search intent.
For category pages, keep it scannable.
