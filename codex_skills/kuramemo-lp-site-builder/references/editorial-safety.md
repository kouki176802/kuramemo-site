# くらメモ 編集・SEO・広告安全ルール

## Editorial rules

- Start with the reader’s problem, not the product name
- Explain why the item/service is timely
- If a foreign trend matters, name the country and reason
- Do not overemphasize “Japan” for normal domestic topics
- Do not claim personal experience unless actually provided
- Separate fact, inference, and editorial judgment
- Include “買わない / 申し込まない方がいい人”

## SEO rules

For every page, check:

- Does the title match a query someone would search?
- Does the H1 clearly state the topic?
- Does the page answer the searcher’s next question?
- Are major competitors/products included even without affiliate links?
- Does the page have enough original comparison judgment?
- Are internal links clear?

Examples:

- 光回線おすすめ比較 2026
- AIスクールおすすめ比較
- メンズではなく全対象のスキンケア比較
- 医療脱毛・美容脱毛比較
- 宅食・冷凍弁当比較

## Trend rules

Use trend evidence to support interest, not to exaggerate.

Good:

- “米国で先に注目された理由”
- “韓国で流行している背景”
- “SNSで見かけるが、買う前に確認したい点”
- “ニュースで話題になったテーマに近い商品”

Avoid:

- A tragedy, disaster, crime, or death as a sales hook
- Unverified viral claims
- “今バズっている” without source or reason

## Affiliate and ad rules

- Always disclose ads clearly
- Approved affiliate links can be used
- Non-approved major competitors should still be included for credibility, using official links when appropriate
- Do not rank by commission alone
- Avoid “最大還元” claims unless conditions are shown
- Link text should say what happens: 公式条件を見る / 申込条件を確認

## Sensitive categories

Beauty:

- Do not guarantee physical change
- Do not diagnose skin or body conditions
- Write about use case, texture, price, official seller, cancellation/return condition

Health:

- Do not claim treatment, prevention, or cure
- Mention supplement overlap and medication caution when relevant

Finance:

- Do not recommend individual securities
- Do not guarantee profit
- Compare fees, tools, account conditions, and risk notes

Telecom:

- Do not guarantee speed
- Compare area, building type, construction, total cost, cancellation, and set discounts

Education / AI school:

- Do not guarantee income, job change, or skill acquisition
- Compare curriculum, mentor support, refund, total cost, and output goal
